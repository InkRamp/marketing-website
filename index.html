About Us

Built with Strong Opinions
About Good Software

i17e was born from a simple frustration: managing sales incentives at scale involves too many spreadsheets, too many manual steps, and too many reconciliation headaches.

In just four months we built a fully working prototype — complete with a responsive UI, a secure and tested API, Auth0 SSO, dual-database support, and data compliance from day one. Not a mockup. A real, running system.

Our stack is deliberately modern and cloud-native: Angular Micro-Frontends on the front-end, AWS Lambda serverless on the back-end, and SST + AWS CDK for infrastructure. Every decision is made with scale, testability, and maintainability in mind.
