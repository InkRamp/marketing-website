The Problem

Excel Was Never Built for This

Managing sales incentives across large field teams in banks and NBFCs breaks down fast when the system is a spreadsheet.

⚠️

Calculation Errors

Manual formula management across hundreds of rows leads to miscalculations, disputed payouts, and eroded trust among your sales teams.

🐌

Processing Delays

Month-end incentive runs take days. Finance, HR, and sales ops are locked in reconciliation cycles that delay payouts and demotivate staff.

🔒

No Transparency

Sales executives have no visibility into how their incentives are calculated. Lack of clarity generates support requests and disputes.

📉

Doesn't Scale

As your team grows from 50 to 5,000 reps across branches, a spreadsheet-based process collapses under the complexity and data volume.

The Solution

A System Built for Scale

i17e replaces your spreadsheet workflow with a purpose-built engine — configurable, auditable, and fast.

01

Configurable Rule Engine

Define incentive rules by role, product, region, or performance tier — without code. Update rules in minutes, not days.

02

Real-Time Tracking

Sales performance data flows through the system continuously. Every rep, lead, and manager sees live progress — no waiting for month-end.

03

Payout Enablement

Automated calculations feed directly into your payroll or payment workflows. Reduce processing time from days to minutes.

04

Audit-Ready System

Every rule change, calculation, and payout is logged with a full audit trail — ready for internal review, compliance checks, or regulatory queries.

How It Works

Four steps from configuration to payout — with no manual intervention required.

1

Define Rules

Admins and leads configure incentive rules by product, geography, or performance band. Rules go live instantly with full version history.

2

Track Sales

Sales activity is captured and mapped against active rules in real time. Every rep's progress is visible on their personalised dashboard.

3

Auto-Calculate

The incentive engine calculates payouts automatically based on your rules. No formulas to maintain. No manual reconciliation.

4

Enable Payouts

Verified results are exported or pushed to your payroll system. The audit trail captures every step — ready for sign-off and compliance review.

i17e was born from a real problem: managing sales incentives at scale in financial institutions involves too many spreadsheets, too many manual steps, and too many reconciliation headaches.

In just four months we built a fully working system — complete with a secure API, Auth0 SSO, a configurable rule engine, dual-database support, and data compliance from day one. Not a mockup. A real, running platform.
